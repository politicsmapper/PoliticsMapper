Management

By Team 14

Introduction

The ability to manage the project was essential in the development of Politics Mapper; we had to keep in contact with our clients, requesting feedback, whilst also organising ourselves and delegating team members in an effective, efficient manner. This section of our report details the delegation of work and the management roles of each team member.

Management

For this project, Anuz was our team leader and so it was his responsibility to maintain contact with our clients, organise bi-weekly meetings and to ensure the project was meeting deadlines. Esben was responsible for overseeing the back-end of the system being developed: the server, ElasticSearch instance and node app, while Reece was put in charge of developing the user interface and merging it with the web application. All team members contributed to reports, presentations and videos in relation to Politics Mapper.
